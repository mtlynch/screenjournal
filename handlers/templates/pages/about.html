ScreenJournal is a way to share and discuss movie recommendations with your friends.

ScreenJournal is private

The ScreenJournal server you join is a private community. Nobody outside of the group can read your reviews or see your ratings.

ScreenJournal is free

ScreenJournal is free to use. There are no ads, and we don't sell your data to anyone.

ScreenJournal is a work in progress

It's still early days for ScreenJournal. It's being actively developed, but right now, it's only a minimal set of features.

Who made this?

ScreenJournal is developed by Michael Lynch.
